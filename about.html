About Us

John Smith is a retired Marine Corps officer and currently works as a biologist. After serving in the military for over 20 years, John decided to pursue a career in the sciences and went back to school to earn a degree in biology.

As a biologist, John is passionate about environmental conservation and spends much of his free time volunteering with local organizations to protect and preserve natural habitats. He is also an avid hiker and enjoys exploring the great outdoors whenever possible.
